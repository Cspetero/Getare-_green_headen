Welcome to
Getare Green Headen Self Help group

We are dedicated to sustainably extracting fibers from banana stems to create a variety of eco-friendly products. Our skilled team works hard to produce high-quality mats, hats, decorative items, and more, all made from the natural fiber of banana stems. In addition to our fiber-based creations, we also produce organic foliar fertilizers, contributing to environmentally responsible agricultural practices. Furthermore, we offer fresh, ripe bananas for sale, supporting both local markets and global sustainability efforts. Explore our products and join us in embracing a greener future!
